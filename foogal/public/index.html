Aapka apna mobile MarketPlace

Your nearby products from your known vendors. Delivered at your place at your pace!!


What are we trying to achieve.
We want to make buying and selling seamless and easy for the people who are located in close proximity to each other at the last mile of the delivery chain. They are the ones who provide the maximum value to the end customer and define "Vocal for Local".

Benefits to the vendors.
Making the nearby vendors, self reliant "Atam-nirbhar". by providing an online platform to the smallest of businesses to showcase and sell their products. The entire platform has been modelled to keep the user experience as simple and as user friendly as possible for both buyers and sellers. The application makes effective use of images from the identifying who the buyer and seller is to listing product and product details. Most of the input is one click based. Effective use of geo tagging has ensured sellers and buyers are able to see products listed on the Foogal marketplace within a radius of 5 km.

Benefits to the buyers.
The buyers will be able to view all items with pictures and prices across vendors from the Item List. They can view products place orders track if it is accepted from the Foogal marketplace also talk to these vendors for any specific instructions.

The application also provides flexibility to increase the geographical area of business by favouritising any Vendor across India, check product prices, place and track orders.
